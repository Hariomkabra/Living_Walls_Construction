## 🌟 About  
**Living Wall Construction** is the official digital platform for a forward-thinking construction company specializing in 
**living walls** – vertical gardens that merge urban spaces with nature. This website showcases their expertise in designing, installing, and maintaining sustainable green infrastructure for residential and commercial clients.  

Key highlights:  
✅ Eco-friendly solutions for modern cities  
✅ Interactive showcase of past projects  
✅ Streamlined client inquiry system  
✅ Mobile-first responsive design  

---

## ✨ Features  
- **Green Portfolio Gallery** - Immersive display of completed living wall projects  
- **Service Breakdown** - Detailed descriptions of design, installation, and maintenance offerings  
- **Instant Contact Form** - Direct communication channel for potential clients  
- **Responsive UI** - Flawless experience across devices  
- **SEO-Optimized** - Built for maximum visibility in search engines
